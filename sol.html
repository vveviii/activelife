Exercise and physical activity are great ways to boost

both your health and energy, improve your overall well-being

and have fun! The health benefits of regular exercise

physical activity are hard to ignore. Everyone benefits

from exercise, regardless of age, sex or physical ability.


Not only can exercise help revent excess weight gain, but it

also helps combat health conditions such as hypertension, arthritis

depression, anxiety, and can significantly lower the risk of

cardiovascular disease (CVD). Furthermore, regular physical

activity also promotes better sleep as well.



Understandably, going to the gym or buying workout equipment can act

as a major disincentive to exercise. However, with the already booming

industry, YouTube has made at-home workouts possible and free!

For anyone too shy to sign up for a high-intensity dance class,

you can simply visit YouTube and browse through the videos to your

heart's content.
